Heat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.
Slice eggplant into 1/4 inch slices. Brush with olive oil and lay out on a sheet pan.
Put eggplant in the oven for 15 minutes. Remove from oven, flip, and put back in the oven for another 15 minutes. Don't rush this part. The eggplant should be very soft and well roasted before being taken out.
While the eggplant in cooking, chop zucchini and add to a sauce pan over high heat with the olive oil. Cook for 5 - 10 minutes until soft. Then add in the marinara sauce. Let simmer for 5 minutes. 
In a small mixing bowl, mix the ricotta and cottage cheese.
Finley grate the parmesan and shred the mozzarella in preparation for assembly.
To assemble: Start with a layer of marinara sauce. Top with layer of lasagna noodles. Spread half the ricotta mixture on top of noodles, then 1/2 of eggplant slices, a third of the parmesan, and a third of the parmesan.
Repeat layers, starting with sauce.
On the next layer, add sauce, noodles, and the remaining mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
It is optimal to let rest 30 minutes before baking.
To bake immediately, let rest for 30 minutes. Then Bake covered for 35 minutes. Remove foil and bake uncovered for additional 15 minutes, until bubbly on top. Optionally burn on broiler another minute or two for browning.
Bake from frozen, let thaw for up to 3 days in the fridge. Let rest on counter for 1 -2 hours before baking. Bake covered for 35 minutes at 350. Check temp. If still cool in the middle, check again after another 10 minutes. Remove foil once warmed through and make for additional 15 minutes. Optionally burn on broiler another minute or two for browning.
